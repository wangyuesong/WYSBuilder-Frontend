PaaS based

Our backend is totally based on Google App Engine. Besides, our building server: Jenkins is based on Google Container Engine, which gives our service high availability and scalability. Front end framework AngularJS and Materialize CSS will give you the smoothest UI experience ever!

Easy to work with

It's super easy to use our service. After your configure a project to build automatically, every time there is a push on your Github project we will start to build automatically and deliver the logs to you.
